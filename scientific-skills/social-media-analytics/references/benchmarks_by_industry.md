# Social Media Benchmarks by Industry

Benchmarks are based on aggregated 2024-2025 data across accounts with 10K-500K followers. All engagement rates are calculated by followers. Posting frequency is expressed as posts per week.

---

## E-commerce / Retail

| Metric | Facebook | Instagram | LinkedIn | TikTok |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Avg Engagement Rate | 0.8% | 1.8% | 1.5% | 5.2% |
| Follower Growth Rate (monthly) | 0.5% | 1.2% | 1.0% | 4.5% |
| Click-Through Rate | 1.2% | 0.8% | 1.0% | 0.6% |
| Posting Frequency (per week) | 5-7 | 5-7 | 3-4 | 5-10 |

**Key Insights**: Product-focused Reels and TikToks significantly outperform static product images. User-generated content (UGC) achieves 2-3x the engagement of brand-produced content. Shoppable posts convert at 1.5-3x the rate of link-in-bio flows.
