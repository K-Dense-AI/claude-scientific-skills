# Caption Templates

Fill-in-the-blank templates for common social media post types. Each template provided in English and Romanian.

---

## 1. Product Launch Post

### English
```
[HOOK: Bold claim or question about the problem your product solves]

Introducing [PRODUCT NAME] -- [one-line description].

Here's what makes it different:
[BENEFIT 1 with emoji]
[BENEFIT 2 with emoji]
[BENEFIT 3 with emoji]

[SOCIAL PROOF: e.g., "Already loved by X early testers" or "X months in development"]

[CTA: e.g., "Shop now -- link in bio" or "DM 'LAUNCH' for early access"]

[HASHTAG BLOCK]
```

### Romanian
```
[HOOK: Afirmatie indrazneata sau intrebare despre problema pe care o rezolva produsul]

Va prezentam [NUME PRODUS] -- [descriere intr-o propozitie].

Ce il face diferit:
[BENEFICIU 1 cu emoji]
[BENEFICIU 2 cu emoji]
[BENEFICIU 3 cu emoji]

[DOVADA SOCIALA: ex. "Deja indragit de X testeri" sau "X luni de dezvoltare"]

[CTA: ex. "Comanda acum -- link in bio" sau "Scrie-ne 'LANSARE' pentru acces anticipat"]

[BLOC HASHTAG-URI]
```

## Template Usage Tips

1. **Never use a template verbatim** -- always customize to your brand voice
2. **The hook is non-negotiable** -- spend 50% of your time on the first line
3. **One CTA per post** -- don't split attention between multiple asks
4. **Adapt length to platform** -- LinkedIn can be longer, TikTok should be shorter
5. **Test variations** -- A/B test hooks and CTAs to find what resonates
6. **Rotate templates** -- don't use the same structure consecutively
7. **Add personality** -- templates are skeletons; your brand voice is the soul
