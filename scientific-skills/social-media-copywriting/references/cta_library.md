## CTA Selection Guide

### By Funnel Stage

| Stage | Goal | Recommended CTA Types |
|-------|------|-----------------------|
| Awareness | Reach, impressions | Share, follow, engagement CTAs |
| Consideration | Education, trust | Save, bookmark, traffic CTAs |
| Decision | Conversion | Shop, book, sign-up CTAs |
| Retention | Community, loyalty | Community, content CTAs |
| Advocacy | Referral, UGC | Tag, share, tell-a-friend CTAs |

### By Content Type

| Content Type | Primary CTA | Secondary CTA |
|--------------|-------------|---------------|
| Educational | "Save this for later" | "Follow for more" |
| Promotional | "Shop now" / "Get yours" | "Share with someone who needs this" |
| Storytelling | "What's your story?" | "Follow along" |
| Behind-the-scenes | "Follow our journey" | "Turn on notifications" |
| Testimonial | "Join X others" | "Book your consultation" |
| Poll/Question | "Vote in comments" | "Share your answer" |
| Giveaway | "Like + follow to enter" | "Tag a friend for extra entries" |

### CTA Placement Rules

1. **One primary CTA per post** - multiple CTAs reduce conversion
2. **Place CTA after value delivery** - earn the right to ask
3. **Match CTA to content** - "save this" for educational, "shop now" for product
4. **Use urgency sparingly** - overuse trains audiences to ignore it
5. **Test CTA variations** - small wording changes can double click rates
