# Platform Format Specifications

> Technical specs, dimensions, and format guidelines for every major social media platform. Updated for 2025-2026.

---

## Instagram

### Image Post (Feed)
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1080 px (square), 1080 x 1350 px (portrait, recommended), 1080 x 566 px (landscape)
- **Aspect ratios**: 1:1 (square), 4:5 (portrait), 1.91:1 (landscape)
- **File types**: JPG, PNG
- **Max file size**: 30 MB
- **Caption length**: Up to 2,200 characters (first 125 visible before "more")
- **Hashtags**: Up to 30 (recommend 8-15 relevant ones)
- **Alt text**: Up to 100 characters (accessibility + discoverability)

### Carousel Post
- **Max slides**: 20
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1080 px or 1080 x 1350 px (consistent across slides)
- **File types**: JPG, PNG per slide; can mix images and videos
- **Video per slide**: Up to 60 seconds
- **Strategy notes**: First slide = hook, last slide = CTA. Consistent visual style across all slides. Use text overlays for scanability.

### Reels
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16 vertical)
- **Duration**: 15 seconds to 90 seconds (sweet spot: 30-60 seconds)
- **File types**: MP4, MOV
- **Max file size**: 4 GB
- **Cover photo**: 1080 x 1920 px (select or upload)
- **Music/Audio**: Instagram music library or original audio
- **Captions**: Auto-generated or manual; critical for accessibility and silent viewing
- **Strategy notes**: Hook in first 1-3 seconds. Use trending audio for boost. Text overlays for retention.

### Stories
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- **Duration**: Up to 60 seconds per story frame
- **File types**: JPG, PNG, MP4, MOV
- **Max file size**: 30 MB (image), 4 GB (video)
- **Interactive elements**: Polls, quizzes, questions, sliders, countdowns, links, mentions, location, music, GIFs
- **Strategy notes**: First frame = hook (people skip quickly). Use interactive stickers for engagement signals. Link sticker for traffic.

### Live
- **Dimensions**: 9:16 vertical
- **Duration**: Up to 4 hours
- **Features**: Live rooms (up to 4 participants), Q&A badges, pinned comments, live shopping
- **Strategy notes**: Promote 24 hours before. Go live when audience is most active. Pin key comments.

## TikTok

### Standard Video
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- **Duration**: 15 seconds to 10 minutes (sweet spot: 21-34 seconds for viral, 1-3 minutes for educational)
- **File types**: MP4, MOV, WebM
- **Max file size**: 287.6 MB (mobile), 500 MB (desktop)
- **Caption length**: Up to 4,000 characters
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 highly relevant ones
- **Strategy notes**: Hook in first 1 second. Loop-friendly endings boost replays. Use trending sounds.

### Photo Mode / Carousel
- **Max photos**: 35
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px recommended
- **File types**: JPG, PNG
- **Duration per photo**: Auto-timed or user-swiped
- **Music**: Can add background music
- **Strategy notes**: Text overlays on each photo. Narrative arc across slides. Strong first image.

### TikTok Stories
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px
- **Duration**: Up to 15 seconds per frame
- **Visibility**: 24 hours
- **Strategy notes**: Quick, casual, behind-the-scenes. Drive to main feed content.

### Live
- **Dimensions**: 9:16
- **Requirements**: 1,000+ followers
- **Duration**: Up to 60 minutes
- **Features**: Gifts, Q&A, co-hosting, live shopping
- **Strategy notes**: Engage with comments. Consistent live schedule builds audience.

### TikTok Series
- **Duration**: Up to 20 minutes per video
- **Access**: Can be gated (paid content)
- **Strategy notes**: For deep-dive educational content. Build anticipation across episodes.

## LinkedIn

### Text Post
- **Character limit**: 3,000 characters
- **Visible before "see more"**: ~210 characters on mobile, ~250 on desktop
- **Formatting**: Line breaks, emojis, bullet points supported. No bold/italic in organic posts.
- **Strategy notes**: First line is the hook. Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences). White space is your friend. End with a question or CTA.

### Image Post
- **Dimensions**: 1200 x 1200 px (square), 1200 x 627 px (landscape), 1080 x 1350 px (portrait)
- **File types**: JPG, PNG, GIF
- **Max file size**: 10 MB
- **Multiple images**: Up to 9 per post (grid layout)
- **Strategy notes**: Native images outperform link previews. Use text-on-image for key stats or quotes.

### Carousel / Document Post (PDF)
- **Format**: Upload as PDF
- **Max pages**: 300 (recommend 8-12 for engagement)
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1080 px or 1080 x 1350 px per page
- **Max file size**: 100 MB
- **Strategy notes**: Most saved format on LinkedIn. First page = hook, each page = one idea, last page = CTA + profile mention. Design as individual slides, export as PDF.

### Video
- **Dimensions**: 1920 x 1080 px (landscape), 1080 x 1920 px (vertical), 1080 x 1080 px (square)
- **Duration**: 3 seconds to 10 minutes (sweet spot: 30-90 seconds)
- **File types**: MP4
- **Max file size**: 5 GB
- **Captions**: Auto-generated (SRT upload available)
- **Strategy notes**: Vertical and square outperform landscape in feed. Add captions -- most LinkedIn scrolling is silent.

### Newsletter
- **Format**: Long-form rich text with images
- **Length**: No hard limit (recommend 500-1,500 words)
- **Features**: Subscriber notifications, SEO indexing, comments
- **Strategy notes**: Consistent publishing schedule. Repurpose into carousel and text posts.

### Poll
- **Options**: 2-4 choices
- **Duration**: 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, or 2 weeks
- **Strategy notes**: Polls get massive reach. Ask genuinely interesting questions. Share results in a follow-up post.

### Article
- **Format**: Long-form with rich media
- **Length**: No limit (recommend 800-2,000 words)
- **Features**: SEO indexed, permanent URL, full formatting
- **Strategy notes**: Lower organic reach than posts but better for SEO and evergreen content.

## Twitter / X

### Tweet
- **Character limit**: 280 characters (free), 25,000 characters (Premium)
- **Strategy notes**: Clarity and wit win. One idea per tweet. Use line breaks for rhythm.

### Thread
- **Max tweets**: 25 per thread
- **Strategy notes**: First tweet = hook. Number each tweet. End with a summary + CTA. Pin the thread.

### Image
- **Dimensions**: 1600 x 900 px (landscape, 16:9), 1080 x 1080 px (square)
- **Max images per tweet**: 4
- **File types**: JPG, PNG, GIF
- **Max file size**: 5 MB (image), 15 MB (GIF)
- **Strategy notes**: Tweets with images get 150% more engagement. Use alt text.

### Video
- **Dimensions**: 1280 x 720 px (landscape), 720 x 1280 px (portrait)
- **Duration**: Up to 2 minutes 20 seconds (free), 60 minutes (Premium), 4 hours (ads/upload)
- **File types**: MP4
- **Max file size**: 512 MB
- **Strategy notes**: Autoplay in feed. First 3 seconds must hook. Captions essential.

### Spaces (Audio)
- **Format**: Live audio rooms
- **Features**: Co-hosts, speakers, listeners, recording
- **Strategy notes**: Regular schedule builds loyal listeners. Record and repurpose into clips.

### Poll
- **Options**: 2-4 choices
- **Duration**: 5 minutes to 7 days
- **Strategy notes**: Binary polls (this or that) drive the most engagement.

## YouTube

### Standard Video
- **Dimensions**: 1920 x 1080 px (16:9, 1080p) or 3840 x 2160 px (4K)
- **Duration**: No limit (sweet spot: 8-15 minutes for ad revenue, 3-5 minutes for engagement)
- **File types**: MP4, MOV, AVI, WMV, FLV, WebM
- **Max file size**: 256 GB
- **Thumbnail**: 1280 x 720 px (custom upload, JPG/PNG, 2 MB max)
- **Title**: Up to 100 characters (recommend under 60)
- **Description**: Up to 5,000 characters (first 2-3 lines visible)
- **Tags**: Up to 500 characters total
- **Strategy notes**: Thumbnail + title = 80% of click decision. Pattern interrupt in thumbnail. Deliver on the promise within first 30 seconds.

### YouTube Shorts
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- **Duration**: Up to 60 seconds
- **File types**: MP4
- **Strategy notes**: Hook in first 1-2 seconds. Loop-friendly. Text overlays for clarity. Use trending sounds.

### Live Stream
- **Dimensions**: 1920 x 1080 px (landscape) or 1080 x 1920 px (vertical for mobile)
- **Duration**: Up to 12 hours (or 24 hours for verified channels)
- **Features**: Super Chat, Super Stickers, memberships, premieres
- **Strategy notes**: Schedule in advance. Engage with live chat. Save as VOD.

### Community Post
- **Formats**: Text, images, polls, GIFs, quizzes
- **Requirements**: 500+ subscribers
- **Strategy notes**: Use to tease upcoming videos, gather feedback, stay visible between uploads.

## Facebook

### Image Post
- **Dimensions**: 1200 x 630 px (landscape), 1080 x 1080 px (square), 1080 x 1350 px (portrait)
- **File types**: JPG, PNG
- **Max file size**: 30 MB
- **Strategy notes**: Native images outperform link posts. Use high-contrast, minimal text on images.

### Video Post
- **Dimensions**: 1280 x 720 px minimum (landscape or vertical)
- **Duration**: Up to 240 minutes
- **File types**: MP4, MOV
- **Max file size**: 10 GB
- **Strategy notes**: Square or vertical for mobile feed. Caption everything. 1-3 minutes optimal.

### Reels
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- **Duration**: Up to 90 seconds
- **Strategy notes**: Cross-post from Instagram Reels. Original content gets priority.

### Stories
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- **Duration**: Up to 26 seconds per frame
- **Features**: Polls, quizzes, music, stickers, links
- **Strategy notes**: Casual, ephemeral content. Good for time-sensitive announcements.

### Carousel (Ads or Organic)
- **Max cards**: 10
- **Image dimensions**: 1080 x 1080 px per card
- **Strategy notes**: Tell a sequential story. Each card should work standalone and as part of the series.

### Live
- **Duration**: Up to 8 hours
- **Features**: Guest co-streaming, reactions, polls, live shopping
- **Strategy notes**: Go live in Groups for targeted reach. Schedule in advance.
