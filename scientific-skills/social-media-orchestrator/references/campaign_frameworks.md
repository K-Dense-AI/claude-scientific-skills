# Campaign Frameworks

Detailed frameworks for planning and executing 6 core campaign types. Each framework provides a structured approach to campaign design, including phases, content strategies, KPI targets, and example timelines.

---

## 1. Product Launch Campaign

**Objective**: Generate awareness, anticipation, and early adoption for a new product or feature.

### Three-Phase Structure

#### Phase A: Tease (Week 1-2)
- **Goal**: Build curiosity and anticipation
- **Content types**:
  - Behind-the-scenes sneak peeks
  - Countdown posts
  - Teaser videos with partial reveals
  - Polls asking audience to guess the product
  - Influencer seeding (send product, embargo until launch)
- **Posting cadence**: 3-4x/week
- **Tone**: Mysterious, exciting, exclusive

#### Phase B: Announce (Week 3-4)
- **Goal**: Maximum reach at launch moment
- **Content types**:
  - Official launch post (hero image/video)
  - Product demo video or carousel
  - Feature highlight series (1 feature per post)
  - Launch day Stories/Reels with live energy
  - Customer testimonial (early access users)
  - Press coverage reshares
- **Posting cadence**: Daily or 2x/day during launch week
- **Tone**: Confident, celebratory, informative

#### Phase C: Sustain (Week 5-8)
- **Goal**: Maintain momentum and drive conversions
- **Content types**:
  - Use-case tutorials
  - Customer success stories
  - FAQ posts addressing common questions
  - Comparison content (vs. alternatives)
  - User-generated content amplification
  - Limited-time launch offers
- **Posting cadence**: 4-5x/week
- **Tone**: Helpful, proof-driven, action-oriented

### KPIs and Targets
| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Reach | 5-10x normal post reach | Platform analytics |
| Website traffic | +50-100% during launch week | Google Analytics |
| Sign-ups/Sales | Campaign-specific target | CRM/E-commerce |
| Share of voice | Increase vs. competitors | Social listening |
| Engagement rate | >5% on launch posts | Platform analytics |

### Platform Focus
- **Instagram**: Hero visuals, carousel features, Reels for demos
- **TikTok**: Behind-the-scenes, unboxing, trend-format reveals
- **LinkedIn**: Professional angle, industry impact, thought leadership
- **Twitter/X**: Real-time updates, launch thread, community Q&A

### Example Timeline (6 weeks)
| Week | Phase | Key Activities |
|------|-------|---------------|
| 1 | Tease | 3 teaser posts, 1 countdown Stories series |
| 2 | Tease | 2 behind-the-scenes, 1 poll, influencer seeding |
| 3 | Announce | Launch day blitz (3 posts), demo video, press reshare |
| 4 | Announce | Feature series (5 posts), testimonials begin |
| 5 | Sustain | Tutorial series, UGC reshares, FAQ post |
| 6 | Sustain | Case study, limited offer, performance review |

## 2. Brand Awareness Campaign

**Objective**: Maximize reach and recognition among target audience. Top-of-funnel focus.

### Strategy
- Lead with educational and entertaining content that delivers genuine value
- Minimize direct selling; focus on being discovered and remembered
- Leverage trending formats to ride algorithm boosts
- Partner with micro-influencers for authentic reach extension

### Content Mix
| Type | Percentage | Examples |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Educational | 40% | How-tos, tips, industry insights, myth-busting |
| Entertaining | 30% | Relatable memes, storytelling, trend participation |
| Inspirational | 20% | Customer stories, mission content, team culture |
| Promotional | 10% | Soft sells, "learn more" CTAs, brand story |

### Content Formats Prioritized
1. Short-form video (Reels, TikToks) — highest organic reach
2. Carousel posts — high save rates, algorithm-friendly
3. Infographics — shareable, educational
4. Stories — daily touchpoint, poll/quiz engagement

### KPIs and Targets
| Metric | Target | Benchmark |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Impressions | 2-5x current monthly average | Platform analytics |
| Reach | +30-50% unique accounts reached | Platform analytics |
| Follower growth | +10-20% over campaign period | Platform analytics |
| Brand mentions | +25% over baseline | Social listening |
| Video views | 1,000+ per Reel/TikTok | Platform analytics |

### Duration
4-12 weeks. Brand awareness is a sustained effort; longer campaigns with consistent output outperform short bursts.

### Platform Focus
- **Instagram**: Primary for visual brands (Reels, carousels)
- **TikTok**: Highest organic reach potential for new audiences
- **Facebook**: Broad demographic reach, group amplification

### Example Timeline (8 weeks)
| Week | Focus | Content |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1-2 | Foundation | Brand story series, introduce content pillars |
| 3-4 | Education | How-to series, tips carousel, industry insights |
| 5-6 | Entertainment | Trend participation, behind-the-scenes, team stories |
| 7-8 | Community | UGC feature, audience Q&A, milestone celebration |

## 3. Engagement Growth Campaign

**Objective**: Build an active, engaged community. Prioritize depth of interaction over reach.

### Strategy
- Focus on two-way conversations, not broadcasting
- Use interactive formats: polls, quizzes, questions, challenges
- Encourage and amplify user-generated content (UGC)
- Create community rituals (weekly themes, recurring series)
- Respond to every comment within 2 hours during campaign

### Content Types
| Format | Purpose | Frequency |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Question posts | Spark conversation | 2x/week |
| Polls and quizzes | Easy participation | 3x/week in Stories |
| UGC reshares | Community recognition | 1-2x/week |
| Challenge/hashtag | Viral participation | 1 per campaign |
| Live sessions | Real-time engagement | 1x/week or biweekly |
| Comment-driven content | "Best comment wins" | 1x/week |

### Community Building Tactics
1. **Create a branded hashtag**: Easy to track, builds community identity
2. **UGC contests**: Photo/video contests with prizes
3. **Ambassador program**: Identify top engagers, give them early access
4. **Weekly series**: "Tip Tuesday", "Feature Friday" — builds habit
5. **Collaboration posts**: Partner with complementary brands

### KPIs and Targets
| Metric | Target | Benchmark |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Engagement rate | 4-6% (up from typical 1-3%) | Platform analytics |
| Comments per post | 2x current average | Platform analytics |
| Saves per post | +50% over baseline | Platform analytics |
| Shares per post | +30% over baseline | Platform analytics |
| UGC submissions | 20+ per month | Hashtag tracking |
| DM conversations | +25% over baseline | Platform analytics |

### Duration
Ongoing, structured in monthly cycles with weekly review checkpoints.

### Platform Focus
- **Instagram**: Stories (polls, quizzes), Reels (challenges), Comments
- **TikTok**: Duets, Stitches, challenges
- **Facebook Groups**: Discussion threads, events

## 4. Lead Generation Campaign

**Objective**: Capture qualified leads by delivering value and directing to conversion points.

### Strategy
- Lead with high-value content that solves real problems
- Use gated content (download in exchange for email) sparingly but strategically
- Every post should have a clear CTA (not always "buy" — often "learn more", "download", "join")
- Create a content funnel: awareness posts feed into lead capture posts

### Content Funnel
```
TOFU (Top of Funnel) — Awareness
  Posts: Industry tips, trend analysis, problem identification
  CTA: "Follow for more" / "Save this"

MOFU (Middle of Funnel) — Consideration
  Posts: How-to guides, case studies, comparison content
  CTA: "Download our guide" / "Join our webinar"

BOFU (Bottom of Funnel) — Decision
  Posts: Testimonials, demos, free trial offers, limited promotions
  CTA: "Start free trial" / "Book a demo" / "Shop now"
```

### Content Mix
| Funnel Stage | Percentage | Content Types |
|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| TOFU | 40% | Tips, trends, educational carousels |
| MOFU | 35% | Guides, case studies, webinar promos |
| BOFU | 25% | Testimonials, demos, offers |

### Lead Magnets
- Free guides, templates, or checklists
- Webinar/workshop registration
- Free tool or calculator access
- Email course or newsletter sign-up
- Free consultation booking

### KPIs and Targets
| Metric | Target | Benchmark |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Click-through rate | 2-5% on CTA posts | Platform analytics |
| Landing page visits | Campaign-specific | Google Analytics |
| Lead capture rate | 15-30% of landing page visitors | Form analytics |
| Cost per lead | Industry-dependent ($5-50 typical) | Ad platform |
| Email sign-ups | Campaign-specific | Email platform |

### Duration
2-6 weeks for focused campaigns; can run as an ongoing program.

### Platform Focus
- **LinkedIn**: Highest B2B lead quality
- **Facebook**: Cost-effective B2C lead gen with ads
- **Instagram**: Visual lead magnets, link in bio strategy

## 5. Event Promotion Campaign

**Objective**: Drive awareness, registrations, attendance, and post-event engagement.

### Three-Phase Structure

#### Phase A: Before (2-4 weeks pre-event)
- **Goal**: Build anticipation, drive registrations
- **Content types**:
  - Event announcement with key details
  - Speaker/performer spotlights
  - Countdown series
  - Early bird offers
  - Behind-the-scenes preparation
  - Testimonials from past events
- **Posting cadence**: 3-5x/week, increasing closer to event
- **CTA**: "Register now", "Save your spot", "Get tickets"

#### Phase B: During (Event day/days)
- **Goal**: Maximize real-time engagement and FOMO
- **Content types**:
  - Live Stories/Reels from the event
  - Real-time quotes and highlights
  - Attendee reactions and UGC
  - Behind-the-scenes moments
  - Live tweets/threads
- **Posting cadence**: Multiple times per day
- **CTA**: "Join us live", "Watch the stream", "Share your experience"

#### Phase C: After (1-2 weeks post-event)
- **Goal**: Extend value, nurture leads, build anticipation for next event
- **Content types**:
  - Highlight reel/recap video
  - Key takeaways and quotes
  - Photo gallery from the event
  - Thank-you posts
  - Survey/feedback request
  - "Save the date" for next event
- **Posting cadence**: 3-4x first week, 1-2x second week
- **CTA**: "Watch the replay", "Download slides", "Join our community"

### KPIs and Targets
| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Registrations | Campaign-specific | Registration platform |
| Attendance rate | 60-80% of registrants | Check-in data |
| Event hashtag usage | 100+ uses during event | Social listening |
| Post-event engagement | 2x normal engagement rate | Platform analytics |
| Post-event sign-ups | 10-20% of attendees | CRM |

### Platform Focus
- **LinkedIn**: Professional events, conferences, webinars
- **Instagram**: Visual events, festivals, product launches
- **Twitter/X**: Real-time updates, live commentary, threads
- **Facebook**: Community events, local events, group engagement

## 6. Seasonal/Holiday Campaign

**Objective**: Capitalize on cultural moments, holidays, and seasonal trends for timely engagement and sales.

### Strategy
- Plan 4-6 weeks before the holiday/season
- Blend brand messaging with seasonal relevance authentically
- Use urgency triggers: limited-time offers, countdown timers
- Adapt visuals to seasonal themes while maintaining brand identity
- Monitor competitor seasonal campaigns for differentiation

### Seasonal Calendar (Key Dates)
| Season/Holiday | Planning Start | Campaign Active | Content Theme |
|---------------|---------------|-----------------|---------------|
| New Year | Early December | Dec 26 - Jan 7 | Fresh starts, resolutions, goals |
| Valentine's Day | Mid January | Feb 1-14 | Love, appreciation, self-care |
| Spring/Easter | Early March | Mar 15 - Apr | Renewal, fresh starts, color |
| Summer | Mid May | Jun - Aug | Outdoor, travel, energy |
| Back to School | Early July | Aug 1-Sep 15 | Preparation, learning, growth |
| Halloween | Early September | Oct 1-31 | Fun, creative, spooky themes |
| Black Friday/Cyber Monday | Early October | Nov 15 - Dec 2 | Deals, urgency, value |
| Holiday Season | Early November | Dec 1-25 | Giving, warmth, gratitude |

### Content Mix
| Type | Percentage | Examples |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Themed value content | 35% | Seasonal tips, gift guides, themed tutorials |
| Promotional | 30% | Limited offers, flash sales, bundles |
| Emotional/storytelling | 20% | Year in review, gratitude, community moments |
| Interactive | 15% | Holiday polls, seasonal quizzes, giveaways |

### KPIs and Targets
| Metric | Target | Benchmark |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Engagement rate | 1.5-2x normal rate | Platform analytics |
| Sales/conversions | Campaign-specific | E-commerce |
| Reach | 2-3x normal post reach | Platform analytics |
| UGC participation | 50+ submissions for contests | Hashtag tracking |

### Duration
1-3 weeks of active campaign, with 2-4 weeks of planning.

### Platform Focus
- **Instagram**: Visual seasonal content, Stories shopping, Reels trends
- **TikTok**: Seasonal trends, holiday challenges
- **Facebook**: Community events, group engagement, local promotions

### Example Timeline (Black Friday — 3 weeks)
| Week | Phase | Content |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Tease | "Something big is coming" teasers, wishlist polls |
| 2 | Build | Early access sign-up, deal previews, countdown |
| 3 | Execute | Daily deals, flash sales, last-chance urgency |
| Post | Wrap-up | Thank-you, shipping updates, Cyber Monday extension |

## Framework Selection Guide

Use this decision tree to select the right framework:

```
What is the primary business objective?
│
├── Launching something new? ──────────> Product Launch
│
├── Growing brand recognition? ────────> Brand Awareness
│
├── Building community? ───────────────> Engagement Growth
│
├── Capturing leads or sales? ─────────> Lead Generation
│
├── Promoting a specific event? ───────> Event Promotion
│
└── Leveraging a cultural moment? ─────> Seasonal/Holiday
```

For hybrid objectives, combine frameworks: e.g., Product Launch + Seasonal (launching during Black Friday) or Brand Awareness + Engagement Growth (new brand building community).
